# Quick Start: Get Your Site on Google

## Why isn't my site showing on Google?

Your website needs to be:
1. **Crawled** - Google's bots need to visit your site
2. **Indexed** - Google needs to add your pages to its search database
3. **Ranked** - Google needs to decide where to show your site in results

## What YOU Need to Do üö®

### Step 1: Verify Your Site with Google (REQUIRED)

**This is the most important step!**

1. **Go to Google Search Console**
   - Visit: https://search.google.com/search-console
   - Sign in with your Google account

2. **Add Your Property**
   - Click "Add Property"
   - Enter: `https://www.barefoothomestays.com`
   - Click "Continue"

3. **Verify Ownership** - Choose ONE method:

   **Method A: HTML File (Easiest)**
   - Google will give you a file like `google1234567890abcdef.html`
   - Download this file
   - Add it to your GitHub repository root folder
   - Commit and push to GitHub
   - Go back to Google Search Console and click "Verify"

   **Method B: DNS (For Domain Experts)**
   - Google will give you a TXT record
   - Add this TXT record to your domain's DNS settings
   - Wait 5-10 minutes
   - Click "Verify"

### Step 2: Submit Your Sitemap ‚≠ê YOU ARE HERE

Once verified (you've completed Step 1! ‚úÖ):

1. **In Google Search Console, look at the LEFT SIDEBAR**
   - Find and click on **"Sitemaps"**
   
2. **In the "Add a new sitemap" text box**
   - Type exactly: `sitemap.xml`
   - (Do NOT type the full URL, just the filename)
   
3. **Click the blue "SUBMIT" button**

4. **Verify Success**
   - You should see "Success" status (green checkmark ‚úÖ)
   - Or "Pending" (will change to Success in a few hours)
   - Discovered URLs will show "7" (may take 24-48 hours)

### Step 3: Request Indexing

1. Click "URL Inspection" in the left menu
2. Enter: `https://www.barefoothomestays.com/`
3. Click "Request Indexing"

### Step 4: Wait for Google

- **1-3 days**: Google starts crawling your site
- **1-2 weeks**: Your site appears in search results
- **2-4 weeks**: Rankings improve

## Need Help?

### Google Search Console Not Working?
- Make sure GitHub Pages is enabled
- Check that your CNAME file has: `www.barefoothomestays.com`
- Wait 24 hours after making changes

### Still Not Showing in Google?
- Check Google Search Console for errors
- Make sure you submitted the sitemap
- Wait at least 2 weeks after verification
- Ensure your site is actually live at www.barefoothomestays.com

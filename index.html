About Us

Modern 1-Bedroom Apartments with Balcony & Full Amenities - Beige, Olive, Dove & Turquoise. Choose from four stylish 1000 sqft apartments(1bhk), each offering the same comfort, space, and modern amenities..

Located just 10-15 minutes from all major safari gates — Bijrani, Dhikala, and Garjia — this is the ideal retreat for those looking to explore the natural beauty of the area while enjoying the comfort of home organic linens, fast Wi‑Fi, and panoramic views. Whether you’re traveling solo, as a couple, or with family, we have the perfect space for you.

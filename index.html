About Us

Our family‑run homestay offers a warm welcome, fresh breakfast, and easy access to hiking trails.

Located just 10 minutes from the town centre, Barefoot Homestays provides a tranquil escape. Each room is designed with comfort in mind, featuring organic linens, fast Wi‑Fi, and panoramic views. Whether you're traveling solo, as a couple, or with family, we have the perfect space for you.

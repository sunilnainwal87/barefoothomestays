About Us

Experience luxury homestay living just 950 meters from Jim Corbett National Park's Garjiya Gate.

Located in Ramnagar, Barefoot Homestays offers spacious, fully-equipped apartments perfect for couples and families seeking a blend of comfort and nature. Each apartment features modern amenities including smart TV, high-speed Wi-Fi, luxury bathrooms with bathtubs, and a fully equipped kitchen. Wake up to mountain views, enjoy home-cooked meals, and experience wildlife right at your doorstep with frequent sightings of deer and peacocks.

Our dedicated caretakers and cook ensure your stay is seamless and memorable. Whether you're here for jungle safaris, peaceful nature walks, or simply to unwind, Barefoot Homestays is your perfect base near Jim Corbett National Park.
